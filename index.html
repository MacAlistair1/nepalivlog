From mountains,

Mardi Base Camp offers an awe-inspiring vantage point to witness the majestic Annapurna South and Hiunchuli Himal up close. These towering peaks, draped in glistening snow, create a mesmerizing panorama against the clear Himalayan skies.

Beautiful Glacier Lakes,

Nestled in the serene Solu region of Dolakha, Dudh Kunda is a breathtaking glacial lake perched at an altitude of 4,780 meters. Surrounded by majestic snow-capped peaks, the lake holds significant spiritual and natural allure, drawing pilgrims and trekkers alike.

Sacred Temples,

Kedarnath, located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand, is one of the holiest pilgrimage sites in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Situated at an altitude of 3,583 meters in the Garhwal Himalayas, it is part of the Char Dham Yatra.
